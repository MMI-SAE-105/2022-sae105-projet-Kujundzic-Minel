What is my project?

My project through this website, remains to pay tribute, to make known, the genocide that lived my country : Bosnia-Herzegovina

Why this project?

Through this project, I wish to make known this history, because very few people know it, and deserves in my opinion to be more known if only on a European scale since it remains a European nation.

What is Bosnia?

Bosnia is a southern European state in the Balkan region. It is surrounded by Croatia to the north, west and south, Serbia to the east and Montenegro to the south. It has a wide opening of about twenty kilometers on the Adriatic Sea. Bosnia-Herzegovina is a federal republic of nearly 3.5 million inhabitants.

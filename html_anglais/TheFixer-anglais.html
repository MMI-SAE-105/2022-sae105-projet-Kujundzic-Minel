It is in the book "The Fix, a history of Sarajevo" that Joe Sacco shares with us the life and thoughts of the people of a country thoughts of the people of a country at war, in order to shed light on this tragedy.

A tragedy that lasted 3 years.

On April 6, 1992, the war in Bosnia-Herzegovina began, provoked by Serbia, with the aim of carrying out "ethnic cleansing", using as a pretext ethnic cleansing", using as a pretext the problems of political power management. political power management. As the war was coming to an end, Joe Sacco travelled to Bosnia-Herzegovina on his own, in 1995, in order to transcribe the memory of this war, by giving a voice to the victims of this conflict, often forgotten by the traditional media.
